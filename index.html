About The Resort

A Retreat Built for
Meaningful Moments

Soni Resort & Farms sits at the foot of the Aravalli mountains in Jhadol, just 90 minutes from Udaipur. Built for those who want more than a venue — a place where your wedding, family gathering, or quiet retreat feels personal, unhurried, and truly memorable.

Eight modern rooms, a pristine lap pool, and sweeping event spaces set against the hills — everything you need, thoughtfully designed and beautifully maintained.
